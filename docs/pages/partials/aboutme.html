About Me

The Story So Far

I'm a game engine and graphics developer from Iran, currently pursuing my Computer Engineering degree at the Iran University of Science and Technology. My journey into game development started with curiosity about how games work under the hood — and quickly evolved into a passion for building everything from scratch.

I specialize in low-level systems programming, graphics rendering, and game engine architecture. From implementing ECS patterns to writing custom ray tracers, I love diving deep into the technical foundations that make games possible.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring Japanese language and culture, breaking down game mechanics, or contributing to open-source projects. I believe in learning by building, and every project is an opportunity to push my understanding further.
