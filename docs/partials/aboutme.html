About Me

The Story So Far

I'm a game developer from Iran, currently pursuing my Computer Engineering degree at the Iran University of Science and Technology. My journey into game development started back in 2015 with Game Maker Studio, where I fell in love with creating simple games. What began as a hobby quickly grew into a lifelong passion.

In 2017, I transitioned to Unity 5, pushing my skills further by joining game jams and competing in school matches. Since entering university, I've been working on more serious game projects, and in 2022 it was published by Medrick, Decorchi, which I continued to refine and update through 2023.

Since 2024, I've been diving deeper into the technical foundations of game development learning graphics programming and engine development. While many of my games remain unpublished or are no longer available, each one has been a stepping stone. I'm still actively learning these low-level concepts while continuing to create new games along the way.
